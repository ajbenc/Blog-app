## ğŸ¤” Common Questions

### Q: Can I delete a post after I've published it?
**A:** Yes! Go to your post and click the pencil icon, or visit your profile to manage all your posts.

### Q: Can I edit a post after publishing?
**A:** Yes! Click the pencil/edit icon on your post. You can edit the text and tags.

### Q: How do I see who liked my post?
**A:** The number next to the heart icon shows total likes. Individual names aren't shown yet (feature coming soon!).

### Q: What's the difference between Feed and Following?
**A:** 
- **Feed**: Shows posts from people you follow + some featured content
- **Following**: Shows ONLY posts from people you follow

### Q: Can I make my profile private?
**A:** Not yet, but this feature is planned for the future!

### Q: How many people can I follow?
**A:** There's no limit! Follow as many people as you want.

### Q: Can I upload multiple images in one post?
**A:** Yes! Click the upload button and select multiple images.

### Q: What file types can I upload?
**A:** 
- **Images**: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP
- **Videos**: MP4, MOV, AVI

### Q: Is there a file size limit?
**A:** Yes, to keep things running smoothly:
- Images: Up to 10MB each
- Videos: Up to 50MB each

### Q: How do I log out?
**A:** Click your profile icon in the top menu, then click "Logout".

### Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?
**A:** Click "Forgot Password" on the login page (password reset feature coming soon!).

### Q: Can I change my username?
**A:** Currently, you can change your display name but not your email. Username changes are planned for the future.
